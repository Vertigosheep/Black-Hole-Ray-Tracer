Physics Explanation Panel

Everything in this project comes from one equation — the null-geodesic orbit equation for light in the Schwarzschild metric:

d²u/dφ² + u = 3Mu²,   u = 1/r
  • Drop the right-hand side and you get simple harmonic motion in u — a straight line in polar form. That's Newtonian optics.
  • Keep it and orbits precess, photons can circle the black hole any number of times, and there's a critical impact parameter b_crit = 3√3 M below which no ray escapes.
  • The photon sphere (r = 3M) is the unstable circular photon orbit; it's why a thin bright ring appears at the edge of the shadow in every render.
  • The event horizon (r = 2M) is where escape becomes impossible regardless of angle — the true edge of the shadow.
