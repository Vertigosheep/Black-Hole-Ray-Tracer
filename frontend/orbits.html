Orbit Simulator

Massive test particles orbiting the black hole, integrated from the general-relativistic orbit equation d²u/dφ² + u = M/h² + 3Mu² — the extra 3Mu² term is what makes GR orbits precess, spiral, and plunge in ways Newtonian gravity never does.
