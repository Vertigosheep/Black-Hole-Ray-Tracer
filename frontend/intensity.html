Light Intensity Profile

Brightness measured along a horizontal scanline through the center of the rendered image, as a function of distance from the black hole's center. Approximated from disk emission and photon-ring focusing, matching the renderer's geometry.

The narrow spike is the photon ring, where light that circled the photon sphere piles up into a thin bright line. The flat zero region is the shadow. Outside the ring, intensity falls off as the accretion disk's emission drops with radius, settling to a dim, uniform background level far from the hole.
