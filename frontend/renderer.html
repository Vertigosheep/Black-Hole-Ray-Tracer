Reading the render

The solid black disk is the shadow — no photon traced backward from inside it can escape. The thin bright ring hugging its edge is the photon ring, light that grazed the photon sphere at r ≈ 3M one or more times before reaching the camera. The warped band is the accretion disk, and its far side appears lifted above and below the black hole because light passing behind it is bent up and over.
